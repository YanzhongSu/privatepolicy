Visa Appointment Booking Assistant - Privacy Policy The Visa Appointment Booking Assistant is a browser extension designed to help users automate the process of monitoring and booking visa appointments. How It Works When you visit the visa booking website, the extension will: Monitor the page for available appointment slots. Automatically book an appointment based on your preferences (if enabled). Notify you of the booking status or any changes in availability. What Data Is Processed The extension processes the following data to provide its functionality: Appointment Details: Dates, times, and locations of available appointments. User Preferences: Your preferred appointment dates, times, and other settings configured in the extension. What Data Is Saved The extension saves the following data locally on your device: User Preferences: Your configured settings (e.g., preferred appointment dates and times). Appointment Information: Details of booked or monitored appointments for your reference. What About Personal Data? No Personal Data Collected: The extension does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or passport details. No Tracking Cookies: The extension does not use tracking cookies or any other means to monitor your browsing activity outside the visa booking website. Data Security All data processed by the extension is handled locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties. The extension does not have access to sensitive information such as passwords, payment details, or personal identification numbers. Third-Party Services The extension interacts only with the official visa booking website to monitor and book appointments. It does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
